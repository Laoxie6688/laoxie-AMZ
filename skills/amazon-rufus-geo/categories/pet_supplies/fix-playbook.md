### Title

优先公式：

`Brand + [piece count] + No Pull Dog Harness Walking & Travel Kit + included core components + core scenes + target size`

避免：

- 只写 all-in-one，不说明包含什么。
- 夸大为 tactical / K9 / extreme heavy puller，除非有真实证据。
- 父体标题里把某个子体颜色/尺码写得过重，造成变体歧义。

### Bullet

推荐 5 条结构：

1. Essential kit：列出高频必需配件。
2. No-pull control：日常牵引、训练、受力。
3. Comfort and fit：neck/chest girth、no choke、padding。
4. Travel safety：seat belt、reflective、bowl、outdoor use。
5. Durability/care/boundary：材质、五金、清洁，以及不适合 extreme puller 的边界。

### A+

必须有：

- 组件-场景对应表。
- 尺寸测量图。
- 竞品类型分流表：basic harness、tactical harness、extra-accessory bundle、own essential travel kit。
- FAQ 模块。

### Q&A

至少覆盖：

1. Is this a complete dog walking and travel kit?
2. How do I choose the right size?
3. Is it good for large dogs that pull?
4. Does it include a car seat belt?
5. Can I use it for hiking and road trips?
6. Is it reflective for night walks?
7. Is it comfortable / no choke?
8. What accessories are included?
9. How is it different from cheaper harness sets?
10. How is it different from tactical harnesses?
11. How is it different from kits with more accessories?
12. Is it washable or easy to clean?
